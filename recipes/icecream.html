Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 14 Fl Oz Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 4 Teaspoons Ube Flavoring Extract
  • 1 Tablespoon Ube Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Pour the cream into a large bowl and begin to whisk. A hand whisk is totally acceptable but if you would like to use an electric mixer, then go for it! After 2-3 minutes, soft peaks form and the cream will begin to hold it's shape.
  2. Add the condensed milk slowly into your bowl. Begin to whip again, thoroughly mixing it into your cream. After another 2-3 minutes stiff peaks will begin to form.
  3. Add in the ube extract and powder into the bowl and begin to mix. Use your discretion on the amount of additional ube to add. If you want a darker color, feel free to add in more ube extract. Before the next step, take a little taste of the mix to ensure it is the sweetness you want!
  4. Pour the mix into a freezer safe container and then let it stay in the freezer for at least 5 hours.
